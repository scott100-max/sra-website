Who We Are

About Scott Ripley

BSc (Hons) MRICS - Managing Director

30+ Years of Industry Experience

Scott Ripley brings over three decades of experience in the quantity surveying and property consultancy profession, having worked his way from apprentice to company director.

His career includes senior roles at leading consultancies including Faithful & Gould, Richard Boothroyd & Associates (Associate Director), and Sweett Group (Project Director - Cost Consultant).

In 2015, Scott founded SRA Property Consultants, successfully growing the business over eight years with a client portfolio including major developers and property companies across Yorkshire and the North of England.

Career Highlights

  • BSc (Hons) Quantity Surveying
  • Member of the Royal Institution of Chartered Surveyors (MRICS)
  • Associate Director by 2004
  • Project Director - Cost Consultant by 2014
  • Founded and led SRA Property Consultants (2015-2023)
  • Delivered projects across residential, commercial, retail and industrial sectors
